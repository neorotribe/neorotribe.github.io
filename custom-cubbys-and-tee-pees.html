Custom Cubbys & Tee Pees

By June Ong
September 22, 2020
0

When we moved into our current home years ago, the previous owner left behind a timber playset. It was perfect for the 3 kids then. They were aged 1, 4 and 7. The kids were on it every single day.

As the kids grow, we realised they were growing out of the playset. They were bored with it. Eventually they were doing dangerous stunts on it, climbing on top of the roof and doing silly things. Liam was very fixated on the idea of a treehouse ever since Brandon shared with him on how he always wanted one growing up. Having a space for Liam to reset his sensory challenges has always been an essential for him too. 

I started researching on the possibility of having a treehouse. But we don't have a tree! Seems pretty unusual because Sydney homes usually have a few at their front or backyards. In Liam's usual fashion, he asked me to grow a tree for it. His mind has no concept on limits. So the next thing was getting a cubby. Most flat packed cubbies are good for toddlers and young kids. Their entry usually would not exceed 1.2m. So our first priority is to have a cubby that would suit them for years, not just at their current age. 

I understand there's a cost involved in having a big investment. So we did it the Neo's way. We saved for it. Our home only consists of legos (mostly given or hand-me-downs), and books. We do not purchase toys which always go into landfills anyway. Our previous big purchase was the trampoline and the kids goes on it every single day.

After almost 6 months of researching and correspondences with different cubby companies in Australia, we finally decided on Custom Cubbys & Tee Pees. It was a thoroughly good experience dealing with owners Darren and Renee. Nothing was too much of a hassle for them. They answered every query I had promptly and professionally. We had to use a freight company as their company was based in Victoria. 

We were kept abreast on the progress of the cubby. The mark of a good company is when owners take pride in what they do. They made sure we levelled the ground properly on our end before the cubby came. It was a smooth process for us even though the cubby was coming from interstate and we had to install it on our own. 

We chose a white fibreglass slide and coordinate the cubby colors based on our home's. It is a visual goal that we will evict the kids eventually to the cubby. 

We will not hesitate to recommend Darren and Renee for their professionalism, workmanshop and heart in what they do.
