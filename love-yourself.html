Love Yourself

June Ong October 19, 2020

I just can't believe the year is coming to a close. I came across a saying that "Sorrow is better than happiness because it has a refining influence" on us. Do you agree?

Safe to say, the whole world is in this together. The Covid situation has been a real mammoth of a crisis this year. Personally, we had many challenges as this crisis is just hallmarked with unpredictability and uncertainties. As a normal human being, we thrive on predictability and we are creatures of habits. Yet the crisis has stripped it off completely. Liam had a hard time and it spillover to the rest of us. 

But looking back, the crisis has helped me see clearly what are the essentials to life. It is a giant reset button pressed on all my priorities. Yes it is important to have your basics covered like a roof over your head and food on the table. Having said that, what truly stands out for me this period is giving love to oneself and others. To love yourself is an art. It is cultivated through time and habit. It is having the wisdom to say no and yes at the right time. To love yourself is the greatest thing you can do for your family. 

In this age we are living, we are torn from all corners, demanding our attention and hearts. Sometimes, we fail to be kind to ourselves. I learnt that no one will do that for me except for myself. When I set aside time to care for myself, I am whole to care for my family, kids and friends so on.

This is my biggest takeback from this year. I am extremely grateful to have my family in lockdown with me. At then end of the day, love is the only currency that will stand the test of life. Love yourself, and love others.
