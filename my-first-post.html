Who Are We

By June
4 September 2020
0

We are Neorotribe. We are a tribe of 2 parents and 3 beautiful children. Our family name is Neo. You would have guessed by now.

Dad is Brandon. Mum is June. Follow by firstborn Liam who is beautiful, kind, unassuming and just so happens to have autism. Second-born Levi is sensitive, free-spirited and marked by his grandma's dimples. And lastly, the princess of our tribe Lily is endearing in every way.Â 

We are from Singapore and have made Sydney Australia our home since 2016. The journey here has been amazing and challenging at the same time. We have been broken and healed, inundated by our experiences. Nonetheless, we would like to think there are also interesting and funny experiences that are worth sharing. We hoped you would be enriched and inspired by our stories.
